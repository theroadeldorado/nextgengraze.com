Have a question?

We may have an answer for you. Here are some of our common FAQ's:

What time can I pickup my board?

Boards can be picked up after 5pm during the week, and before 12pm on the weekends. Flexibility for pickup may be available, please just ask. Delivery is also available based on availability. Fees do apply.

What will be included on my board, and what is it served on?

Boards and boxes may vary based on what is locally and seasonally available, and they are always served on a palm leaf platter or disposable platter, unless otherwise noted.

How and when will I know my board can be accommodated.

A text will be sent with confirmation 24-48 hours after the order is placed.
